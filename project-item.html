Play and learn Играме и учиме

I am still working on this project.

This project was started to contribute to children with autism. It is about small games that will help children express themselves and at the same time they will learn new things through play. Many of the games in this web site include clicking or movment with the mouse. Through all pages there are soft and yet playful colors compatible for children with autism.

Thesee game are ideal for early education in the comfort of the home. Parents have full control over performances of their children. Also they can make schedule for their kids which will be easy to follow and helpful throug out the day of the child.

The website has three main sections

  • Play - home page where are all games in categories.
  • Schedule - page where child can see the schedule made by his parent.
  • Parent - difeerent page which is avalible just for the parent.
The most important thing is for children to have fun during playing the games and get more comfortble during interacting and expressing themself.

Working on this project is just me. I am using HTML5 CSS and JavaScript. I am finished with home page for now, and working my way up to the games. Also this website is only on my mother language, Macedonian.
